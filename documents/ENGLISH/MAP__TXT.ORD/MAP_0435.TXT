ÝSTART  004     USE YELLOW NAVSTONE
YOU PLACE THE YELLOW NAVIGATION STONE IN THE STRANGE MACHINE AND
IMMEDIATELY A GENTLE HUM SPREADS THROUGH THE SHIP. A SHORT TIME LATER
IT LIFTS OFF THE GROUND AND YOU FLY TOWARDS THE YELLOW MOON OF LYRAMION.
ÝEND..

ÝSTART  005     USE GREEN NAVSTONE
AS SOON AS YOU PUT THE MACHINE INTO OPERATION WITH THE GREEN NAVIGATION STONE
A GENTLE HUMMING SOUND STARTS. A LITTLE LATER THE SHIP LIFTS OFF INTO THE
AIR BY ITSELF AND YOU FLY TOWARDS THE GREEN MOON OF LYRAMION.
ÝEND..

ÝSTART  006     USE BLUE NAVSTONE
YOU INSERT THE BLUE NAVIGATION STONE INTO THE MATCHING OPENING IN THE
STRANGE MACHINE. THERE ARE A FEW CLICKS AND IT AL GOES QUIET AGAIN.
APART FROM THAT, NOTHING AT ALL HAPPENS.
ÝEND..
