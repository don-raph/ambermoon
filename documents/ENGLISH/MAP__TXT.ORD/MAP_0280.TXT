ÝSTART  007     REG APPEARS

"ARE THE KITCHEN STAFF ASLEEP? WHY ARE YOU NOT IN THE PAN? I AM HUNGRY!"^

"NO MATTER, RAW MEAT IS SAID TO BE VERY HEALTHY. WILL DO REG GOOD!"^
